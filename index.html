Options

Find Your Perfect Match

Explore different electric vehicle options tailored to your lifestyle.

Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV)

Pure electric vehicles powered solely by batteries, offering zero emissions and the simplest driving experience.

  • 100% electric power
  • Zero tailpipe emissions
  • 200-400 mile range
  • Lower maintenance costs
Learn More

Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV)

The best of both worlds, with electric-only operation for daily commutes and gas backup for longer trips.

  • Electric + gasoline power
  • 30-50 mile electric range
  • No range anxiety
  • Reduced emissions
Learn More

Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEV)

Self-charging hybrids that blend gasoline and electric power for improved efficiency without plugging in.

  • Self-charging
  • No plugging required
  • Improved fuel economy
  • Lower emissions

Infrastructure

Charging Simplified

Everything you need to know about keeping your electric vehicle powered up.

Home Charging Solutions

Charge conveniently at home overnight with Level 1 (standard outlet) or Level 2 (240V) chargers, providing 2-6 miles of range per hour of charging.

  • Convenient overnight charging
  • Level 2 chargers provide up to 44 miles of range per hour
  • Available tax incentives for installation
  • Lower electricity rates during off-peak hours

Public Charging Network

Access over 100,000 public charging stations nationwide at shopping centers, parking garages, hotels, and other convenient locations.

  • Extensive network coverage
  • Many locations offer free charging
  • Mobile apps help find available stations
  • Compatible with most electric vehicles

DC Fast Charging

Rapid charging for longer trips, providing 80% charge in just 20-30 minutes at strategically placed charging stations along major highways.

  • 80% charge in 20-30 minutes
  • Enables long-distance travel
  • Growing infrastructure
  • Multiple connector standards (CCS, CHAdeMO, Tesla)
